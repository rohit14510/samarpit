About Us

Samarpit Jankalyan Foundation: Empowering Youth Through Sports and Values

100.5K
Worldwide Donor
Success Case 90%

Samarpit Jankalyan Foundation, Jhajjar, Haryana, is dedicated to empowering youth through sports. We nurture young talent with values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance. Offering training in football, cricket, basketball, and athletics, supported by modern facilities and expert coaches, we aim to create national and international champions. Through events and scholarships, we ensure underprivileged youth get equal opportunities, building brighter futures through the power of sports.
